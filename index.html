Ever found yourself painting your fence & wondered if you could find someone to help? Remember that time when your friend was gonna help you move but bailed out when something else came up? What if you are clearing out your garage and could use an extra hand? There are hundreds of odd jobs that you wish you could hire someone to do it but didn't know where to go & whom to trust. Odd Jobs List helps you find someone that you can rely on backed by reviews from others in your area. So whether it is raking leaves, shoveling your driveway, chopping a tree in your backyard, picking up your drycleaning, loading a truck or just about anything, you can rely on the Odd Jobs List.

It is never easy finding someone you can trust, more so for jobs that don't require any specific qualifications. There are a lot of tasks that you wish you could pay someone to do them for you but just could'nt find anyone. In most cases you ended up doing them yourself unable to find someone trust worthy. Odd Jobs List bridges the gap by helping you find people that you can trust. You post a job and tag it appropriatele so that it is searchable. Someone looking for a job then searches in categries they wish to offer their services and proxoimity to their zipcode. If they are available for the dates mentioned in the posting, they apply for the job with a brief description of the services they offer.

You then can look up the replies and read reviews for every applicant. If you find someone you like, you can contact them and mark teh posting as closed. This way you find people you can trust based on reviews from others in your area. All futher communication is offline and depending on the amount of work you pay them in cash or kind. You can then leave feedback about the applicant which will then help someone else and helps the system get better.

FAQ

What does the bowler hat in the logo stand for?
It is a reference to one of the most famous James Bond villains from the movie Goldfinger.
Is it a free service?
The service will be free through beta and we hope to monetize it once we iron out the kinks. We are working on a payment model where we would charge a monthly fee for number of postings.
Why can't I just go to craigslist?
Nothing's stopping you from doing so. Localization & trust are what we're basing our service on. Our aim is to conect you with someone who has worked for others in your area. You read the reviews and there is a good chance one of those is by someone you know. Now that is something you won't find elsewhere.
